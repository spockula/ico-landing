Pre-Sale Frequently Questions

What are the objectives of this token?

NRT is a free floating token of Nifty Row protocol and it plays a role in stabilizing the marketplace and the governance of the protocol. NRT is required for conducting voting for the approval of the transfer of music, movies and artworks NFTs from fractional investment marketplace to auction marketplace.

When is Token Private-Sale ?

The Token private sale starts on 7th June 2021 and will conclude by the 7th July in two rounds Private A and Private B with each running for a fortnight consecutively.

When is the Public-IDO start date?

The Public-IDO starts immediately after the conclusion of the Private-Sale and will last for 7 days.

What are the advantages of the NRT Token

The NRT token will enable token holders to stake and borrow on the Nifty Row’s DeFi service while also having the option to insure their NFT collectibles. It also enables transactions on the market place at a discounted rate of 0.75% on fees. This means that as the adoption and demand for the use of Nifty Row increases, there will be a positive impact on NRT.

How may I take part in Private-sale?

In order to part take in the private sale one must register through the private sale Whitelist Submission Form and provide required information. This ensures we enforce a good range of criteria to deliver the most qualified whitelist of accredited investors from across the globe.
